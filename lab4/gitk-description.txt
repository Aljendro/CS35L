1. The box on top shows all the branches
   on the left. The commit messages in
   right next to each head as well as the
   author of the commits with the date
   when the commit was placed
2. On the bottom left we see that a
   better summary of the commit. It
   contains the change log ( a summary
   of all the files that changed during
   the commit) and the files that were
   changed in the commit
3. On the bottom right we have a handy
   shortcut or table of contents that
   allows us to click and see the
   sections.
